New Weapon

Outlaw

What is the Outlaw? VALORANT's newest weapon, the Outlaw, is a Sniper Rifle. The weapon has high wall penetration and deals significant damage in even body shots. It is more expensive than the Marshal but is cheaper than the Phantom and the Vandal.

Valorant is a free-to-play tactical shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. The game was announced with the codename Project A in October 2019 and was released on June 2, 2020.
